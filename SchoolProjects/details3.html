Android Blackjack Game

Project

Create a Blackjack application using Java in Android Studio Arctic Fox | 2020.3.1 Patch 2.

Final Product

A mobile app with three tabs: one to add your name, one for the main game, and one for credits. Five face down cards are shown for the player and five are show for the house. Player gets their two cards revealed when they click start game and they are able to hit until they reach a total of five cards revealed. If the player reaches the five card limit or they hit stay, the house reveals cards until they bust or beat the player. Points are adjusted accordingly.

Learning

Fragments were used to handle the activities, so knowledge of how to manage fragments was gained. Shared preferences were researched and used to store player names, and object-oriented principles were further practiced by creating unique classes to handle the cards and the players.
