Bootstrap Storefront

Project

Design and create a storfront using the Boostrap front-end framework

Final Product

A single-page store showcasing Dungeons and Dragons dice. Contains a nav bar, a front page with a carousel and modals for products, an about page, and a contact page with a contact form. Divs toggle between hidden and visible based on what section you are looking at to allow all content to be in one html document.

Learning

Obtained a good knowledge of common Bootstrap elements like forms, buttons, modals, and carousels. Gained a better knowledge of design principles as well, as all design and style choices were made by me.
