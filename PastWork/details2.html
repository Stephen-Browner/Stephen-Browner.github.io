Small Business - Monthly Report Automation

Problem

Monthly reports for a local business were taking entire 12-hour days to put together. This process involved collection sales reports from 4 different software programs, reading the data, and entering it manually.

Solution

Automate this process using scripts to manipulate data tables and fill a master table with all the complied information.

Outcome
