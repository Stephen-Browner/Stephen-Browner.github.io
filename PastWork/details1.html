Goot Robot Brewing Co. - Shopify

Problem

In response to the initial lockdowns faced due to COVID-19 in 2020, this local Halifax brewery went quickly to work creating an e-commerce platform using Shopify. Due to a lack of a technical lead and many hands working on the website, it quickly became cluttered and disorganized.

Solution

I was brought on to look through the website and make adjustments. I had a knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which allowed me to navigate the code with more understanding than other staff.

Outcome

Through communication with one of the managers and the e-commerce lead at the time, we were able to come up with a few specific areas of focus. The main area being the general layout of the store. I was able to undo a lot of the changes that had been made previously and instate some more robust styling rules to keep the grids in-line. Additionally, I added new features for advertising and identification checks, and worked with a marketing team member to create a page for their brand and values to be displayed.

Learning

I was able to familiarize myself with the inner workings of the Shopify platform, which I did not know anything about previously. Additionally, I was able to further develop communication and project management skills by having to set meetings and progress reports with members of different teams.
